About

I am a DataX Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Operations Research and Financial Engineering (ORFE) and the Center for Statistics and Machine Learning (CSML) at Princeton University, where I work with Dr. Jason M. Klusowski. I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from Penn State University under the supervision of Dr. Bharath K. Sriperumbudur and co-advised by Dr. Bing Li.

Research Interests

  • Non-parametric statistics
  • Reproducing kernel spaces in probability and statistics
  • Statistical learning theory
  • Optimal transport
  • Decoding strategies in large language models
